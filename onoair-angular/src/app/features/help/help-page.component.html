Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply search for your desired destination, select a flight, and click the "Book" button.

 Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be canceled up to 14 days before departure. Visit the 'Manage Booking' section to cancel or modify your flight.

 How do I check my flight status?

You can check flight status by entering your flight number on the homepage or in the 'Flight Status' section.

 What is ONO Air's baggage policy?

Passengers are allowed one carry-on and one checked bag. Additional baggage may incur extra fees. Refer to our 'Baggage Policy' section for detailed weight limits and charges.

 How can I select my seat?

Seats can be selected during the booking process. Alternatively, visit 'Manage Booking' to choose or upgrade your seat after purchase.

 Does ONO Air offer in-flight meals?

Yes, ONO Air offers complimentary meals on international flights. For domestic flights, snacks and beverages can be purchased during the flight.

 When should I check in for my flight?

Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 3 hours before the flight. Please arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights.

 What documents are required for international flights?

For international travel, you must carry a valid passport and any required visas. Some countries may require additional documentation. Please check our 'Travel Documents' section for specific requirements.

 Can I travel with my pet on ONO Air flights?

Yes, ONO Air allows small pets in the cabin. Larger pets must be transported in the cargo hold. Review our 'Pet Travel Policy' for more details and restrictions.

 How can I join ONO Air's loyalty program?

You can sign up for our loyalty program on the ONO Air website under the 'Rewards' section. Members earn points for every flight and enjoy exclusive benefits.

 How do I redeem my loyalty points?

Loyalty points can be redeemed for flight upgrades, free tickets, and additional baggage. Visit the 'My Rewards' section to redeem your points.

 What happens if my flight is delayed or canceled?

In case of delays or cancellations, ONO Air will notify you by email and SMS. You can rebook or request a refund via 'Manage Booking' or by contacting our support center.

 How do I request special assistance at the airport?

Special assistance can be requested during booking or at least 48 hours before your flight through the 'Accessibility Services' section.
